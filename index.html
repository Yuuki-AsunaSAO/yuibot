Yui

An S.A.O Inspired Discord bot

My features

I am currently able to:

Keep track of player levels in a server, or a server category
Let users fight creatures
Give users warnings and kick them upon reaching x amount of warnings
Show users a neat profile embed, with their level, XP, rep points and much more!

Commands list

Commands or arguments within square brackets are bot-owner only!
yui help | Sends you a DM with the commands
yui roadmap [add|remove]| Planned new features are listed here
yui hi | Make yui greet you! [yui say] | Make the bot send a message in the current channel
yui fight (enemy) | Attack a certain monster
yui fight | Continue attacking a monster
yui profile | Show your current level/exp/balance and your reputation points. (broken!)
yui report (user, reason) | Report a certain user with a specific reason. (broken!)
